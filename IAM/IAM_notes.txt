IAM
- Centralized control of your AWS account
- Shared Access to your AWS Account
- Granular Permissions
- Identity Federation(Active Directory, FB, Linked, ect)
- Multifactor Authentication
- Provide temporary accesses for user/devices and Services
- Allow setup password policy
- Integrates with many different AWS services
- Supports PCI DSS Compliance

Critical Terms
- Users
- Groups of users with same permissions
- Roles:  cration of roles then assign to resources
- Policy: Document that defines on or more permissions (assigned to user, groups, roles)
